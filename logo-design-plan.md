# BrightByte Berlin - Logo Design Plan

## Brand Context
- **Name:** BrightByte Berlin (3 B's)
- **Industry:** Web development studio
- **Target:** Small businesses
- **Theme:** "Stand Out. Get Found. Grow." / Spotlight
- **Colors:** Dark theme, amber/gold accent (#FBBF24)
- **Personality:** Modern, minimal, professional, tech-savvy, premium

## Requirements
- Modern and clean
- Abstract but recognizable
- Unique and ownable (not generic icons)
- Scalable (works at 16px favicon to large)
- Meaningful connection to brand
